About Us

Sprout & Spark is dedicated to providing hands-on, inclusive workshops for families with children who have Special Educational Needs (SEN). Our mission is to create safe, creative spaces where children can develop gross motor skills, social connections, and confidence, while families find a supportive community. Donations and fundraising efforts help keep our services accessible to everyone.
