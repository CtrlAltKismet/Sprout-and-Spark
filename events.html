Events

Here are our upcoming events near you!

Our events are fully inclusive for SEN needs. We will always have ear defenders, fidget toys, quiet rooms and trained staff available to support all learning needs.

Painting Workshop (6+)

Children can explore colours, textures, and creativity with brushes, sponges, and sensory-safe paint. No artistic skill needed - just a willingness to have fun!

Accessibility Features:

  • Sensory-friendly paints (low-odour, hypoallergenic)
  • Noise-reducing mats to help lower sensory overload
  • Visual choice boards to support non-verbal children
  • Quiet zones for breaks
  • Trained staff to assist with any needs

Location Support:

  • Calm, natural-light rooms
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Quiet chill-out zone nearby for children needing breaks
  • Sensory-friendly fidget toys
  • Adjustable easels and seated options available

Key Details:

  • Ages: 6+
  • Type: Creative / Fine Motor Skills
  • Time: 10:00 - 11:30
  • Date: 04/05/2025
  • Duration: 90 minutes
  • Group size: Small groups (max 8 children per room)

Drawing Workshop (Ages 6+)

A gentle introduction to sketching with pencils, charcoal, and pastel crayons. Focuses on expression rather than the "perfect" results!

Accessibility Features:

  • Weighted pencils and grips for easier handling
  • Simple step-by-step visual guides
  • Noise-cancelling headphones always available (for any workshop)
  • Visual choice boards to support non-verbal children
  • Quiet zones for breaks

Location Support:

  • Calm, natural-light rooms
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Well-spaced tables for personal space
  • Sensory-friendly fidget toys
  • Optional soft background music

Key Details:

  • Ages: 6+
  • Type: Visual Arts / Emotional Expression
  • Time: 10:00 - 11:00
  • Date: 03/05/2025
  • Duration: 1 hour
  • Group size: Small groups (max 8 children per room)

Textiles Workshop (Ages 8+)

Children learn basic sewing, weaving, and fabric art using tactile-friendly materials. Focuses on building fine motor skills and creativity.

Accessibility Features:

  • Adaptive sewing tools (large-eye needles, finger guards)
  • Weighted lap pads for calming support and focus during sewing
  • Flexible pacing (no pressure to finish projects)
  • Quiet zones for breaks
  • Trained staff to assist with any needs

Location Support:

  • Calm, natural-light rooms
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Quiet chill-out zone nearby for children needing breaks
  • Soft, padded work tables
  • Clear written, verbal and visual instructions

Key Details:

  • Ages: 8+
  • Type: Hands-on-Crafting / Fine Motor Development
  • Time: 09:00 - 10:30
  • Date: 27/04/2025
  • Duration: 90 minutes
  • Group size: Small groups (max 6 children per room)

Woodwork Workshop (Ages 10+)

Hands-on introduction to basic woodworking skills like sanding, hammering, and building simple projects such as birdhouses and planters. ADULTS MUST ATTEND WITH CHILDREN.

Accessibility Features:

  • Child-safe tools with easy grips

  • Noise-buffered stations (reduced loud hammering noise)
  • Quiet zones for breaks

Location Support:
