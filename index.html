Welcome to Sprout & Spark!

At Sprout & Spark, we believe that every child deserves a space to grow, create, flourish and feel understood. We run free, hands-on workshops designed especially for families with children with Special Educational Needs (SEN), offering activities like painting, clay modelling, and wood carving that help build gross motor skills, encourage social interaction, and most importantly -- bring joy. Our workshops are for children ages 6 and up, with more advanced workshops aimed at older children.

Our sessions are welcoming, inclusive, and thoughtfully designed with sensory-friendly features to support neurodiverse children and their families. Alongside the creative fun, Sprout & Spark is a place to connect -- a growing community where parents and carers can find support, share experiences, and feel a little less alone.

Take a look at our workshops!

We have a variety of different workshops currently available for the family to enjoy. You can find more information on our events page.
